Innovation Partners Launches Bridge4Hope™, a Charitable Foundation

Bridge4Hope™ is the charitable foundation of Innovation Partners, LLC

The mission of Bridge4Hope™ is to bridge the gap between need and hope for our neighbors across the street and around the world. We aim to raise funds and awareness in support of organizations working to make a difference, both locally and globally.

Imagine heading home from high schoolâ€¦ but you have no home. Up to 10 teens at each local suburban high school have no place to sleep at night. We often think of homelessness as something we find in more urban areas, but kids are homeless in suburbs, too.

Via the fundraising project, Bridge4Hope™ will be supporting a partnership between the Open Hands Foundation and The Bridge for Youth to create "Hope House" — a new shelter and crisis center for teenage kids in the southwestern Minneapolis-St. Paul area. The goal of this partnership is that no youth in the community should be without a safe, stable place to sleep at night. With your help, they will be opening Hope House in Fall of 2015. The Bridge for Youth will provide all services for youth, including:

24-hour crisis hotline Emergency shelter for youth ages 10-17 Walk-in and by-appointment counseling for youth, parents, and families Drop-in teen health clinic Weekly, drop-in support groups

The Bridge4Hope™ objective is to raise $10,000 in donor contributions to support the important work of Hope House -- and each donation will be matched dollar for dollar up to up to $10,000 by Bridge4Hope™.

We invite you to join us in supporting the effort to provide a safe place for homeless teens to sleep and receive supportive services. And remember — for every dollar you give, Bridge4Hope™ will double your impact by matching your donation.
